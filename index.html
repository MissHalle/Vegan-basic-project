🐝 What is Veganism? Veganism is a lifestyle when you choose to not eat, wear, or use animal products! 🦈

🌱 Veganism is the practice of excluding all animal products; dairy (think yogurt, milk, and cheese), any and all meat of any animals (even fish), aggs, and honesy. People who go into veganism do so for many reasons, here are some popular reasons:

  • 🌎 Enivornmental impact
  • 🐮 Animals wellbeing
  • 🥳 Personal choice

So even though you may not drink cow milk or eat animal meat as a vegan, you can still enjoy a wide variety of milk or meat alternatives made by plants and this is true for all non-vegan to vegan options! This is the mind set to replace animal products for the animals! 🍄
